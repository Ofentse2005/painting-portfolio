Painting is a visual art, which is characterized by the practice of applying paint, pigment, color or other medium to a solid surface.The medium is commonly applied to the base with a brush. Other implements, such as palette knives, sponges, airbrushes, the artist's fingers, or even a dripping technique that uses gravity may be used. One who produces paintings is called a painter
